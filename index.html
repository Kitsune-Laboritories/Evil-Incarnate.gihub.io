WHat are we making?

We at Kitsune Labs are making a game called Evil Incarnate where you control a cat who;s purpose is to cause as much destruction and mayhem as possible in their small city by knocking things over, breaking things apart along with many other activities that cats will get up to

The Progress So Far

We are currently in the process of working on the layout of the city where the game will be taking place aswell as the player controls, the modeling for various items that will be present in the city along with the main character of our game. Additionally we now have a title screen.
